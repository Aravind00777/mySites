The Talent Management division UTM looks to match emerging actors to the right talent agencies. We work with the top agencies in the world and in addition we are developing our internal agency for clients who do not yet have representation.

Our showcase feedback is provided monthly by guest agents and casting directors at the some of the most established and prestigious agencies in the UK and US.

We are very proud to say that all of our signings have trained and emerged from the school, which we feel is proof of our dedication to excellence and opportunity.

Unseen Talent Management Clients

(This list is UTM clients and does not include all actors signed from our monthly showcases)

Archie Renaux Unseen Talent Management/The Artists Partnership/Luber Roklin

Former student turned Unseen management client Archie Renaux is currently playing lead role of Mal in Netflix's Shadow and Bone. He recently starred in The World's Greatest Beer Run opposite Zak Efron, Bill Murray and Russell Crowe as well as Catherine Called Birdy for Amazon. Archie made his debut starring in Gold digger for BBC 1 alongside Ben Barnes.

Solly McLeod Unseen Talent Management/CAA/ The Artists Partnership

Former student turned Unseen management client Solly Mcleod is currently playing a main role in upcoming Viggo Mortensen western The Dead Don't Hurt. Solly has recently starred in House of The Dragon, The Rising, Boxing Day and will be playing the titular lead in Mammoth's upcoming Tom Jones for ITV and PBS.

Annabella King Unseen Talent Management/ The Artists Partnership

Current student Annabella King has recently starred in Red, White and Royal Blue alongside Uma Thurman and Stephen Fry. She will shortly be appearing in The Haunting of Helen's Bridge.

Rocky Gharu Unseen Talent Management / Denton Brierley

Rocky has recently starred in Gassed Up for Amazon and will be appearing in a new highly anticipated show for Channel 4 as well as Sally Wood's upcoming feature Hooked.
