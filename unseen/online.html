How we use the online medium to enhance our weekend classes

The Unseen is committed to the importance of in-person learning and we maintain a policy of our core learning taking place onsite.

Free midweek sessions for in-person weekend students!

Saying that, the online medium is tremendously efficient in terms of bolstering a part time course and encouraging midweek work and practice. So from January 2021 we are providing extra training for free using the online medium! These bonus sessions will include specialised topics such as camera technique from The Unseen tutors as well as interesting and motivational masterclasses with industry specialists.

The Unseen Showcase

The monthly Unseen showcase online continues to lead to meetings and agency signings and our viewing partners have increased dramatically over the past year. Regular second round feedback on all tapes comes from Heather Basten casting, The Artists Partnership, casting directors at excellent agencies including Nina Gold casting and Luber Roklin Entertainment in the US.
